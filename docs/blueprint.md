# **App Name**: Talent Tango Puzzle Master

## Core Features:

- Student Quiz Registration: Allow students to register with their name, email, and course to start the quiz, with their details persisted.
- Timed Multi-Type Quiz Interface: Present a series of mental ability and puzzle questions, including multiple-choice and open-ended formats, within a predefined time limit.
- Real-time Timer Display: Visually indicate the remaining time for the quiz to students with timely alerts.
- Automated Quiz Scoring: Automatically calculate the student's score for objective questions, including deductions for incorrect answers based on configured penalties.
- Student Result Summary: Display a comprehensive summary of the student's performance upon quiz completion, including total score and qualifying status.
- Admin Dashboard for Student Performance: Provide a secure administrative interface to view all student quiz submissions, individual scores, and access detailed answer breakdowns for review.
- AI-Powered Open-Ended Answer Analysis Tool: As an admin, use an AI tool to analyze and provide feedback or determine the correctness of student textual responses for subjective questions.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color will be a rich, professional indigo (`#4426D9`). This deep hue is chosen to evoke a sense of knowledge and calm focus, anchoring the intellectual nature of a puzzle and mental ability test.
- The background will be a subtle, almost imperceptible lilac-tinted white (`#F3F2F5`), ensuring clarity and reducing eye strain while subtly harmonizing with the primary indigo.
- A vibrant blue (`#1156DC`) will serve as the accent, dynamically drawing attention to calls-to-action, active elements, and important highlights, providing a refreshing contrast against the primary and background tones.
- Both headlines and body text will utilize 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font known for its modern, clean, and highly readable appearance across various screen sizes. This choice emphasizes clarity and a neutral, objective presentation vital for an assessment platform.
- Maintain the use of clean, clear, and contextually relevant icons for navigation (e.g., home, login) and functional elements (e.g., admin actions). Favor simple, modern line-art or filled icons for consistency.
- Embrace a clean, spacious, and responsive layout. Centralize key content areas to maintain user focus on quiz questions and administrative data. Employ clear delineation for sections like questions, forms, and results to enhance usability across devices.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for modals, button hovers, and state changes to provide a polished and engaging user experience without distracting from the core functionality.
